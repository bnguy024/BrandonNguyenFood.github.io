Shio-Negi Buta-don (塩ネギ豚丼)

A perfect mix of Umami & Pork

A simple yet elgant dish that incorporates the unique flavors of Japanese Cusine with pork and rice.
It is definitely one of my favorite dishes when it comes to Japanese food and it can be made in a matter of minutes.
Preparation is easy and a cook of any skill level can make this dish.

Ingredients
1 Japanese Long Green Onion
2 Cloves of garlic
1/4 Sliced Lemon
1 Tbsp Sesame Oil
1 Tbsp Japanese Cooking Sake
1 Tbsp Salt and Pepper
100g steamed white rice
Instructions
1. Finely chop up the long green onion and 2 cloves of garlic.
2. In a skillet, heat 1 tbsp of the sesame oil and add in the all finely chopped garlic.
3. Once the garlic has released its fragence, On medium heat add in the thinly sliced pork belly.
4. Once the pork has lightly cooked through, add in the finely chopped long green onion.
5. Add in 1 tbsp of Japanese Cooking Sake, 1 tbps of Salt and pepper. Cook until the Pork has a light char.
6. Once done, squeeze 1 sliced lemon over the pork and serve over hot steamed rices
