About Me

My name is Brandon Nguyen and I am a 4th year computer science student at the University of Calgary.
Born and raise in Calgary with nothing to do, I've was drawn to Japanese culture from a very young age.
Wanting toexperience Japan first hand I decided to study abroad in Kyoto for one year. During my study
abroad, I learned the true taste of Japanese food. There I learned that there is more food diversity and began
eating my way around Japan.

Food Philosofy

I believe that food experiences should be shared and enjoyed by all. I believe that what us westerns know
about Japanese food is very limited and I want to let the world know about the lesser known Japanese dishes.
There is unique diversity when it comes to Japanese food. They take influence from various regions and my it
their own. Although these dishes are not famous, I am certain that with one bite everyone will be instantly hooked.
